Currently, I am a third-year PhD student advised by Prof. A. Erdem Sarıyüce in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University at Buffalo. Before that, I received my B.S. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Shahjalal University of Science and Technology, Bangladesh in 2018.

My research interests are broadly in Graph Mining, Graph Management, Machine Learning, Data Mining, Network Science and Graph Learning. Currently, I am working on Graph Mining, Network Robustness, and Critical Node/Edge Identification in complex networks.
